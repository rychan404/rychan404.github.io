About Me

I am a computer programmer at heart with a passion for tinkering prototypes and ideas to become fully-fledged products and solutions, whether it would be devices to help students with anxiety or entertaining videos to make people smile. What started as a child's simple exploration on my dad's laptop became a hunger and love for challenges that allow me to apply myself to creating something that matters. My expertise lies in the intersection between technology and creativity - leveraging as a way to discover endless possibilities to a problem. I believe in the idea of collaboration and connecting with others by taking feedback and applying it to projects that matters most.
